2022 Prince George's County Maryland Trash Tracker

What does this website do?

  • Helps visualizes the amount of trash in Prince George's County, giving the user an idea of how much trash is produced in a year and where are the point where there is lots of trash. Users are able to filter it out on the specific organization that reported it and have collected the trash in the area. Also how much trash bags they have collected in that area. Users can play around with the map to see how much trash is near them. Also each point on the map has information of the trash collected, getting information like the organization, latitude, longitude, and much more.

Data Provided by the LitterTRAK API Link to LitterTRAK API
